Jun 2024 — MiCA Enforcement

EUR stablecoins dipped as non-compliant tokens (EURT) were delisted, then surged as EURC/EURS captured the vacuum. EUR market cap doubled in 12 months post-MiCA (+102%).

Oct 2025 — JPYC Launch

Japan's first regulated yen stablecoin under the Payment Services Act. JPY market cap jumped from ~$10M to $34M in 6 months — the fastest-growing non-EUR currency peg.
